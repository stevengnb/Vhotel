ABOUT US

VhoTel is a legendary hotel in Jakarta that has been providing impeccable service to its guests since 1999. The founders aimed to set a benchmark in the industry by building the hotel with an assortment of rooms and top-notch facilities like spas, gyms, and pools, which were not commonly found in hotels at the time.

VhoTel's dedication to customer service helped it establish a loyal clientele, which included both business and leisure travelers. As Jakarta grew into a bustling metropolis, VhoTel continued to thrive as one of the city's premier hotels by investing in continuous improvements and renovations to keep up with the changing times and the evolving needs of their guests.

At our hotel, we believe in going above and beyond to exceed our guests' expectations. From personalized recommendations to thoughtful amenities, we strive to create an atmosphere of warmth, comfort, and hospitality that will make our guests feel right at home.
